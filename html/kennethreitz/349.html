Sarah MERRICK, eldest daughter of Thomas MERRICK and Sarah STEBBINS [Family], was born on 05/09/1643 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts. Sarah died in 1712 in East Haven, New Haven, Connecticut aged about 69.
