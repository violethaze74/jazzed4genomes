Judith "Judah Indah" ROCHE, 5th child of John ROCHE and Elizabeth "Ann" DUNN [Family], was born on 12/25/1804 in Easton's Corner, Wolford, Grenville, Upper Canada. Judah Indah died on 12/04/1886 in Merrickville, Lanark, Ontario, Canada aged 81.
