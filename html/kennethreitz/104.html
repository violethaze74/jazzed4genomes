Johann BROSIUS, 2nd child of Johan George BROSIUS and Anna Catherine SIMON [Family], was born in 1731 in Weierbach, Rheinland, Germany. Johann died in 1794 in Upper Mahoney, Northumberland, Pennsylvania, USA aged about 63.
