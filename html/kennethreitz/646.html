Matthis ALGEIER1, only child of Paulus ALGEIER, was born in 1550 in Altenstadt, Cham, Bayern, Germany. Matthis died on 11/03/1594 in Fellbach, Rems-Murr-Kreis, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany aged about 44.

Margaretha SCHNEITMANN2, only child of Jerg SCHNEITMANN and Elisabeth UNKNOWN, was born in 1559 in Germany. She died on 11/12/1594 in Fellbach, Rems-Murr-Kreis, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany aged about 35.

On 08/18/1579 Matthis married Margaretha. One son and one daughter given.

Children

i    

Lady Appollonia ALLGAIER3 was born on 03/09/1585 in Germany.

  
ii    

Gregorius ALLGAIER4 was born on 03/13/1592 in Fellbach, Rems-Murr-Kreis, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany. He died on 11/11/1648 in Kirchheim, Esslingen, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany aged 56.
