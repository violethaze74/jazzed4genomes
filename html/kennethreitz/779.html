John ROCHE1 was born about 02/1766 in Wexford, County Wexford, Ireland. John died about 1847 in Canada West aged about 81.

Elizabeth "Ann" DUNN2 was born on 08/01/1779 in Wexford County, Ireland. She died on 11/13/1868 in North Crosby, Leeds, Upper Canada aged 89.

He had a family with Ann. 2 sons and 3 daughters given.
