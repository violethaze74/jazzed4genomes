William MERRICK1, eldest son of William MERRICK and Sylvia COMSTOCK, was born on 03/28/1793 in Brockville, Leeds And Grenville United Counties, Ontario, Canada. William died on 04/19/1867 in Merrickville, Ontario aged 74.

Judith "Judah Indah" ROCHE2, 5th child of John ROCHE and Elizabeth "Ann" DUNN, was born on 12/25/1804 in Easton's Corner, Wolford, Grenville, Upper Canada. She died on 12/04/1886 in Merrickville, Lanark, Ontario, Canada aged 81.

On 11/09/1826 William married Judah Indah. 5 sons and 3 daughters given.
