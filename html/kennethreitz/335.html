Mary MERRICK, 3rd child of Thomas MERRICK and Sarah STEBBINS [Family], was born on 09/28/1645 in Springfield, Massachusetts. Mary died on 07/28/1648 in Springfield, Massachusetts as an infant.

== Biography ==. {{Died Young}}Mary Merrick was born September 28, 1645. She was the daughter of [[Merrick-302|Thomas Merrick]] and [[Stebbins-1|Sarah Stebbins]]. Her parents had another daughter Mary on August 27, 1647, suggesting that this Mary died prior to that date.<ref>The Settlers of the Beekman Patent, V.1-8, page 741.
