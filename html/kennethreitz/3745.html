1. == Biography ==Jacob Hepler was born in 1788 in Upper Milford Township, Northampton,Pennsylvania, United States (now Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States). He was the son of [[Hepler-138|Johan Casper Hepler Junior]] and [[Schmidt-5815|Anna Maria (Schmidt) Hepler]]. He passed away in Upper Mahantongo Township, Berks, Pennsylvania, United States (now Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States).
