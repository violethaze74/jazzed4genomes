Husband Johann HEPLER b. 1650 d. 1720
Wife Margaretha KELLER b. 11/22/1646 d. 03/02/1690
Child Johann HEPLER b. 1680 d. 1746

Johann HEPLER1, only child of Johann HEPLER, was born in 1650 in Benningen, Ludwigsburg, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Johann died in 1720 in Benningen, Ludwigsburg, Baden-Württemberg, Germany aged about 70.

Margaretha KELLER2, only child of Johannes KELLER, was born on 11/22/1646 in Bissingen, Esslingen, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. She died on 03/02/1690 in Benningen, Ludwigsburg, Baden-Württemberg, Germany aged 43.

On 02/15/1676 Johann married Margaretha. One son given.

Children

Johann HEPLER3 was born in 1680 in Bissingen, Esslingen, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. He died in 1746 in Lomersheim, Enzkreis, Baden-Württemberg, Germany aged about 66.
