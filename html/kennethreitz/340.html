Miriam MERRICK, 2nd child of Thomas MERRICK and Elizabeth TILLEY [Family], was born on 05/01/1656 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts. Miriam died on 10/11/1684 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts aged 28.
