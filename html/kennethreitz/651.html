Husband Lt William ALLIS b. 1613 d. 09/06/1678
Wife Mary BROWNSON b. About 1615 d. About 1681

Lt William ALLIS1 was born in 1613 in Dagenham, Essex, England. William died on 09/06/1678 in Hartford, Hartford, Connecticut aged about 65.

Mary BROWNSON2 was born about 1615 in Braintree, Norfolk, Massachusetts. She died about 1681 in Hatfield, Hampshire, Massachusetts aged about 66.

On 06/26/1678 William married Mary. No children given.

[[Category:Puritan_Great_Migration]] {{Puritan Great Migration}} [[Category:Braintree%2C_Massachusetts]] == Biography == William Allis was born 1613 probably in Dagenham, Essex, England, butpossibly London. He emmigrated to Mass. about 1635. Some sources say he was with Winthrop's fleet in 1630 on the Mayflower in its third voyage. He took the Freeman's Oath at Braitree, Mass. in 1640. His first wife was Mary, born 1614 in Braintree, Essex, England, whom he marriedabout 1641 in Braintree, Norfolk, Mass., and they had eight children.His second wife was Mary Brownson, whom he married June 25, 1678 in Hatfield, Hampshire.
