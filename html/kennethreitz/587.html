Hannah WATSON, eldest daughter of John WATSON and Mary PEARSON [Family], was born in 1831 in Renwick, Cumberland, England, United Kingdom.

== Biography ==. In 1841 censusHannah Watson was the daughter of John and Mary Watson of Scales Houses Street Renwick Cumberland. She was 10 years old. Her father was a Stonemason. Her grandfather Anthony Watson was living with them he was81 born in 1760 and also a Stonemason. In 1891 Hannah Watson 59 is living at HighBank Hill farm with her niece Mary Jane Watson who is 13 years old.
