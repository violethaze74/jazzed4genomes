Catherina HEPLER, eldest daughter of Johann HEPLER and Anna SCHMIDT [Family], was born on 01/23/1779 in Upper Milford, Northampton, Pennsylvania, United States. Catherina died in 1830 in Mahontongo, Schuykill, Pennsylvania, United States aged about 51.

== Biography ==. Catharina was the daughter of Johan Casper Hepler and Anna Maria Schmidt. She was the wife of George Kehler. Family links:. Parents:. Johan Casper Hepler (1751 - 1816).
