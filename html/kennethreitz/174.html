Thomasine "Tamazine" FROST, 7th child of Edward FROST and Thomasine BELGRAVE [Family], was born before 08/11/1600 in St James, Stanstead, Suffolk, England. Tamazine died on 06/13/1654 in Sudbury, Middlesex, Massachusetts Bay aged 53.
