John MERRICK, 7th child of William MERRICK and Judith "Judah Indah" ROCHE [Family], was born on 07/03/1837 in Merrickville, Lanark, Upper Canada. John died on 09/10/1920 in Merrickville, Lanark, Ontario, Canada aged 83.
