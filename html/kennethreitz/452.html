Johan REITZ, eldest child of Johan-Peter REITZ and Magdalena WEYGAND [Family], was born on 07/30/1723 in Lohrhaupten, Main-Kinzig-Kreis, Hessen, Germany. Johan died in 1763 in Whitehall, Lehigh, Pennsylvania aged about 40.
