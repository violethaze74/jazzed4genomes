Lewis JODON1, only child of Francis JODON Jr. and Rebecca IDDINGS, was born on 07/05/1853 in Spring Twp, Centre County, PA. Lewis died on 06/29/1917 in Spring Twp, Centre County, PA aged 63.

Jemima TIBBENS2, only child of Samuel TIBBENS, was born on 03/24/1852 in Marion Twp, Centre County, PA. She died on 09/30/1922 in Philipsburg, Centre County, PA aged 70.

In 1877 Lewis married Jemima. One son given.
