1. [[Category: Stanstead, Suffolk]] == Biography == {{England Sticker}} {{Died Young}}Philemon Whale was born to Philemon Whale and Elizabeth Frost November 1625, in Stanstead, Suffolk, England. He was christened November 25, 1625, Stanstead, Suffolk, England. He died on December 23, 1625 in Stanstead, Suffolk, England.
