Elizabeth HAWKS, only child of Sergeant John HAWKS and Alice UNKNOWN [Family], was born after 09/22/1697 in Deerfield, Franklin, MA. Elizabeth died on 02/29/1704 in Canada aged 6.

[[Category: Raid on Deerfield]]. == Biography ==Daughter of John Hawks (1643 - c 1722) & Alice Hawks. She was taken captive during the French & Indian attack on Deerfield, the "Deerfield Massacre" on 29 Feb 1703/4. She was slain en route to Montreal, Canada.
