Capt. John MERRICK, eldest son of Thomas MERRICK and Elizabeth TILLEY [Family], was born on 11/09/1658 in Springfield, Massachusetts Bay Colony. John died on 04/10/1748 in Springfield, Hampshire County, Massachusetts aged 89.
