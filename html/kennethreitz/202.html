Elisha HAYWARD, 7th child of Thomas HAYWARD and Susanna TOWNE [Family], was born about 1642 in Duxbury, Plymouth Colony. Elisha died in 1703 in Bridgewater, Plymouth County, Province Of Massachusetts Bay aged about 61.
