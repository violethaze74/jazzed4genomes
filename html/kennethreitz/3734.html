Sebastian BROSIUS, eldest child of Johan George BROSIUS and Anna Catherine SIMON [Family], was born about 1725 in Weierbach, Rheinland, Germany. Sebastian died about 01/03/1791 in Upper Mahoney, Northumberland, Pennsylvania, USA aged about 66.
