Johann Peter SIMON1, son of Adam SIMON and Eva STRAUS, was born on 12/25/1645 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. Johann died on 03/13/1703 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rheinland, Preußen aged 57.

Maria KIEFFER2, only child of Peter KIEFFER and Unknown MARGARETHA, was born on 08/08/1646 in Enkirch, Bernkastel-Wittlich, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. She died on 03/10/1676 in Sulzbach, Birkenfeld, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany aged 29.

On 06/17/1662 Johann married Maria. 2 sons and 3 daughters given.
