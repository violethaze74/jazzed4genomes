William MERRICK, eldest son of William MERRICK and Sylvia COMSTOCK [Family], was born on 03/28/1793 in Brockville, Leeds And Grenville United Counties, Ontario, Canada. William died on 04/19/1867 in Merrickville, Ontario aged 74.
