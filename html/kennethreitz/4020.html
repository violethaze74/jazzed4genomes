Later removed to Williamstown, Dauphin county,for 8 years and served as school director of the township. In 1876-1877 hestage between Williamstown and Tower City. He was associated with hisfatherin the general store business at Trevorton for 2 years before his father's death, after which he carried on a meat market. In 1893 he went to Chillisquaque twp and engaged in market gardening (provided all of thevegetables for Bucknell University).
