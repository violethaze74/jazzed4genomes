William ROCHE, eldest child of John ROCHE and Elizabeth "Ann" DUNN [Family], was born about 07/17/1796 in Upper Canada. William died on 12/07/1841 in Eastons Corners, Merrickville-Wolford, Leeds And Grenville, Canada West aged 45.
