Ensign John HOWARD Jr., eldest child of Lt. John HOWARD and Martha HAYWARD [Family], was born about 1653 in Plymouth, Plymouth Colony. John died before 10/03/1726 in Bridgewater, Plymouth, Province Of Massachusetts Bay aged about 73.
