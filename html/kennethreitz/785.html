Johan Abraham SCHNEIDER1 was born on 01/17/1685 in Herrensulzbach, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. Johan died on 10/31/1724 in Aabef, Herrensulzbach, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany aged 39.

Anna Catherine SIMON2, 3rd child of Hans Adam SIMON and Anna Catherine HOEHN, was born on 09/26/1692 in Unterjeckenbach, Rheinland, Germany. She died in 1750 in Berks, Pennsylvania aged about 58.

On 04/18/1712 Johan married Anna. One son and one daughter given.
