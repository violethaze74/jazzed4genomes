1. [[Category: Stanstead, Suffolk]] == Biography == {{England Sticker}}Mary Frost was born in 1606/7 to Edward Frost and Thomasine Belgrave in Suffolk, England. She was baptised on 21 January 1607 in Stanstead,Suffolk, England, possibly at [http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/stanstead.htm St James] church. Mary Frost married John Skot in 1622 in Stanstead, Suffolk, England, possibly at [http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/stanstead.htm St James]. The death date stated as 1622 has not been proved. ==Research Notes== There is a John Scott baptised November 22, 1622 in Ipswich, Suffolk.Father is listed as John Scott, but no mother listed.
