Anna HEPLER, 13th child of Johann HEPLER and Anna SCHMIDT [Family], was born on 11/04/1797 in Northumberland County Pennsylvania. Anna died on 10/12/1869 in Schuylkill County Pennsylvania aged 71.

== Biography ==. Birth: Nov. 4, 1797. Northumberland County. Pennsylvania, USA. Death: Oct. 12, 1869. Schuylkill County. Pennsylvania, USA. Anna was the daughter of Johannes Hepler and Maria Catherina Maurer. She was the wife of (1) Emanuel Christ and (2) Frederick Snyder. Anna Maria Hepler was born 4 November 1797 and baptized at Himmel's Church at Rebuck, Northumberland County, on 20 February 1798; baptism sponsors were Casper Hepler and his wife Anna Maria (Schmidt).
