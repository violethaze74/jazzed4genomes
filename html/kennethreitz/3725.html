2. == Biography ==Born about 1604 in Wrexham, County Denbigshire, Wales<ref name=Ancestry/> "Randall Buttall signed a will on 15 June 1680. Randall bequeathed the house in which he resided, the gardens, etc. to his daughter Abigail, and the remainder of his houses to Samuel, the eldest son of Nathaniel Buttall, who was Randall's eldest son. He willed one shilling each to his sons Nathaniel, Samuel, Jonathan, and Charles, daughter Rebecca, and son-in-law Dr. Thomas Wynne. It seems interesting to note there is no indication of anything being bequeathed to his daughter Martha, who was married to Thomas Wynne, who did receive something. He had hisestate probated in 1684 in Saint Asaph, Denbighshire, Wales, United Kingdom.
