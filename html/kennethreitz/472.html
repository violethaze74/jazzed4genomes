Salome REITZ, 12th child of Andreas REITZ and Maria-Margaretha "Anna" BROSIUS [Family], was born on 02/10/1805. Salome died about 02/1836 in Rebuck, Northumberland, Pennsylvania, United States aged about 31.

== Biography ==. [[Category:Alsatian Tryons in America]]. [[category: Northumberland County, Pennsylvania]]. Salome died probably at the end of February 1836 and was buried in the Himmel Churchyard on 1 March 1836.<ref>Pennsylvania and New Jersey, Church and Town Records, 1669-2013; Historical Society of Pennsylvania; Philadelphia, Pennsylvania; Reel: 236 (Ancestry.com)</ref> Her headstone names her as Salome Reitz, wife of Michael Tryon. Her birthdate is also given as 10 Feb 1805. The raised level of the ground obscures the next line(s) of the inscription, but the date 1836 can be read. == Sources ==. <references />* [https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/30204314 Salome Reitz Tryon's Find a Grave Memorial 30204314].
