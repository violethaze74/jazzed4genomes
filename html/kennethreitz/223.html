Jacob HEPLER, 7th child of Johann HEPLER and Anna SCHMIDT [Family], was born on 10/26/1788 in Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States. Jacob died on 06/15/1809 in Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States aged 20.

== Biography ==Jacob Hepler was born in 1788 in Upper Milford Township, Northampton,Pennsylvania, United States (now Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States). He was the son of [[Hepler-138|Johan Casper Hepler Junior]] and [[Schmidt-5815|Anna Maria (Schmidt) Hepler]]. He passed away in Upper Mahantongo Township, Berks, Pennsylvania, United States (now Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States).
