Thomas MERRICK, son of Thomas MERRICK and Sarah STEBBINS [Family], was born on 04/12/1641 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts. Thomas died in 1664 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts aged about 23.

== Biography ==. {{Died Young}}Thomas was the third child born in Springfield, Massachusetts. He wasborn in 1641, and died young, possibly in 1644 or 1645, but almost certainly sometime between 1641 and 1651. He was the son of [[Merrick-302|Thomas Merrick]] and [[Stebbins-405|Sarah Stebbins]].
