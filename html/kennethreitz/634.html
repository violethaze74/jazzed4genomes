Thomas WYNNE, eldest child of Jonathan WYNNE and Sarah GREAVES [Family], was born in 1695 in Blockley Township, Philadelphia County, Pennsylvania, British America. Thomas died on 11/27/1757 in Boston, Suffolk, Massachusetts, British America aged about 62.
