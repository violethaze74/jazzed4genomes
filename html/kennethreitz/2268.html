1. == Biography == Birth: Jan. 4, 1775 Lehigh County Pennsylvania, USA Death: Oct. 8, 1842 Pitman Schuylkill County Pennsylvania, USA Peter M. Hepler was born in Upper Milford Twp., Northampton (now Lehigh) Co., PA. He was the son of Johan Casper Hepler, Jr. and Anna Maria Schmidt. He married Anna Maria Wagner abt 1796 in Berks Co., PA. Peter died in Upper Mahantongo Twp., Schuylkill Co., PA. He was buried at St. Jacob's (Howerter's) Lutheran and Reformed Church Cemetery in Line Mountain, Northumberland Co., PA.
