Christopher HEPLER, 3rd child of Johann HEPLER and Anna SCHMIDT [Family], was born on 07/10/1777 in Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States. Christopher died on 10/18/1847 in Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States aged 70.

== Biography ==Christopher Hepler was born in 1777 in Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States. He was the son of [[Hepler-138|Casper Hepler Junior]] and [[Schmidt-5815|Anna Maria (Schmidt) Hepler]]. He married Catharina (Wagner)Hepler on October 31, 1799 in Berks, Pennsylvania, United States. Together, they were the parents of Benjamin Wagner Hepler (1800 - 1869), Magdalena (Hepler) Geist (1803 - 1869), Catharine (Hepler) Haas (1805 - 1892), Anna Maria (Hepler) Howerter (1808 - 1876), George WashingtonHepler (1810 - 1848), Elizabeth (Hepler) Geist (1813 - 1888), Daniel Wagner Hepler (1815 - 1894), John Wagner Hepler (1818 - 1898), and Joel Wagner Hepler (1825 - 1860). Christopher Hepler passed away in 1847 in Upper Mahantongo (area now part of Eldred Township) Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States.
