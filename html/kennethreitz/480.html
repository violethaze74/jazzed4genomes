Edward RICE, 2nd child of Henry RICE and Elizabeth FROST [Family], was born about 1608 in Stanstead, Suffolk, England. Edward died after 1616 aged about 8.

== Biography ==Edward Rice, the son of [[Rice-220|Henry Rice]] and [[Frost-76|Elizabeth Frost]], was probably born in April or May 1608 and was baptized at St. James Church in Stanstead, Suffolk, England in May 1608.<ref>Mary Lovering Holman, "English Notes on Edmund Rice." ''The American Genealogist'' 10, (1943): 134. Baptism of "Edward Ryce the son of Henry Ryce."</ref>. Edward is specifically mention in the will of his grandfather, [[Frost-72|Edward Frost]], dated 26 July 1616 (proved 4 October 1616).<ref>Holman, "English Notes on Edmund Rice," 135.</ref>. == Sources ==. <references />. == See Also ==*Holman, Mary Lovering.
