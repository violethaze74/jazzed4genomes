William MERRICK1, 2nd child of William MERRICK and Judith "Judah Indah" ROCHE, was born about 1826. William died on 05/06/1900 in Athens, Ontario aged about 74.

Jane BECKETT2, only child of Thomas BECKETT and Margaret ROATH, was born on 06/18/1834 in Kemptville, Ontario. She died on 10/03/1926 in St. Catherine's, Ontario aged 92.

He had a family with Jane. 3 sons and 2 daughters given.
