Johann Christoph HEPLER, eldest child of Johann HEPLER and Anna Margaretha KRAMER [Family], was born on 02/03/1709 in Vaihingen An Der Enz, Württemberg, Deutschland. Johann died on 08/18/1750 in Northampton, Northampton, Pennsylvania aged 41.
