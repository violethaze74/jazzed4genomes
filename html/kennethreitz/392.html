Hannah MOORE, eldest daughter of Joseph MOORE and Lydia "Lydiah" AXTELL [Family], was born on 01/02/1673 in Sudbury, Middlesex, Massachusetts. Hannah died on 10/28/1730 in Sudbury, Middlesex County, Massachusetts aged 57.

[[Category: Sudbury, Massachusetts]]. == Biography ==. Hannah Moore was born 2 Jan 1673 in Sudbury, Massachusetts, daughter of Joseph and Lydia (Maynard) Moore.<ref>Sudbury Vital Records, Births, p 94.</ref>. The will of her father, Joseph Moore, mentions his daughter Hannah Gleason. This identifies her as the Hannah Moore who was married to Joseph Gleason in Sudbury, Massachusetts, in 1705. <ref>''New England Historical and Genealogical Register'', [https://www.americanancestors.org/databases/new-england-historical-and-genealogical-register/image/?volumeId=11719&pageName=302&rId=241132382 Vol. 57 (1903), page 302]</ref> Joseph Gleason and Hannah Moore were married at Sudbury, Massachusetts, on 17 February 1705.<ref>Vital Records of Sudbury, Massachusetts, [https://www.americanancestors.org/databases/massachusetts-vital-records-to-1850/image/?volumeId=7806&pageName=200&rId=142142245 page 200]</ref>. The death of Joseph Gleazon, Jr., was recorded at Sudbury, Massachusetts, on 15 October 1711.<ref>Vital Records of Sudbury, Massachusetts, [https://www.americanancestors.org/databases/massachusetts-vital-records-to-1850/image/?volumeId=7806&pageName=305&rId=142141296 page 305]</ref> His wife, Hannah Gleason, survived him. <ref>Middlesex County, Massachusetts, Probate Files [http://www.americanancestors.org/databases/middlesex-county-ma-probate-file-papers-1648-1871/image/?pageName=9204:2&volumeId=14472&rId=263866076 Papers for Joseph Gleason, 1711]</ref> Hannah Gleason, widow, died at Sudbury on 28 October 1730.<ref>VitalRecords of Sudbury, Massachusetts, [https://www.americanancestors.org/databases/massachusetts-vital-records-to-1850/image/?volumeId=7806&pageName=305&rId=142141296 page 305]</ref>. == Sources ==. <references />.
