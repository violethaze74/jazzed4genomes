Johann Peter SIMON1, son of Adam SIMON and Eva STRAUS, was born on 12/25/1645 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. Johann died on 03/13/1703 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rheinland, Preußen aged 57.

Maria MEYER2, only child of Jost MEYER and Margaretha KIEFFER, was born on 08/08/1639 in Enkirch, Bernkastel-Wittlich, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. She died about 1692 in Sulzbach, Birkenfeld, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany aged about 53.

On 08/09/1676 Johann married Maria. 3 sons given.
