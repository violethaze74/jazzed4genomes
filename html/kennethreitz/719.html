Foster JODON Jr.1, only child of Lewis JODON and Jemima TIBBENS, was born on 07/05/1878 in Near Bellefonte, Spring Twp, Centre County, Pennsylvania. Foster died on 08/10/1946 in Pennsylvania Ave, Centre Hall, Centre County, Pennsylvania aged 68.

Cora BROOKS2, only child of Mary GROSS, was born on 07/16/1877 in Pleasant Gap, Spring Twp, Centre County, PA. She died on 11/05/1959 in Bellefonte, Centre County, PA aged 82.

On 07/31/1900 Foster married Cora. One daughter given.
