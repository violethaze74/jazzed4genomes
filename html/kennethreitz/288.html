Sarah MARSHFIELD, 3rd child of Thomas MARSHFIELD and Mercy UNKNOWN [Family], was born on 08/25/1633 in Berry Pomeroy, Devon, England. Sarah died on 03/09/1709 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts aged 75.
