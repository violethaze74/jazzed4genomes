Joseph MOORE, 3rd child of Joseph MOORE and Lydia "Lydiah" AXTELL [Family], was born on 08/01/1671 in Sudbury, Middlesex, Massachusetts Bay. Joseph died on 11/18/1753 aged 82.

== Biography ==. Joseph Moore was born on 1 August 1671 in Sudbury, Middlesex, Massachusetts.
