Catharina REITZ, eldest daughter of Andreas REITZ and Maria-Margaretha "Anna" BROSIUS [Family], was born on 10/24/1786 in Washington, Northumberland County, Pennsylvania. Catharina died on 06/25/1854 in Washington, Northumberland County, Pennsylvania, USA aged 67.

== Biography ==Catharina ([https://images.findagrave.com/photos/2009/78/30256898_123753898149.jpg Catharine] or Catherine) was born in 1786. She was the daughter of Andreas (Andrew) Reitz (1755 - 1837) and Maria Margaretha Brosius Reitz (1761 - 1826).
