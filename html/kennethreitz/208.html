Susanna "Susannah" HAYWARD, 4th child of Thomas HAYWARD and Susanna TOWNE [Family], was born on 01/10/1632 in Aylesford, Kent, England. Susannah died on 11/04/1661 in Bridgewater, Plymouth, Massachusetts aged 29.
