George BAUDER1, only child of Daniel BADER and Lady Appollonia ALLGAIER, was born in 1620 in Marktoberdorf, Allgau, Baden-Wurttemberg, Germany. George died in 1701 in Marktoberdorf, Allgau, Baden-Wurttemberg, Germany aged about 81.

Rosina RENN2, only child of Oswald RENN and Barbara DER LEHNEN, was born in 1622 in Lehnen, Tyrol, Austria. She died in 1701 in Marktoberdorf, Germany aged about 79.

On 04/22/1652 George married Rosina. 5 sons and 2 daughters given.
