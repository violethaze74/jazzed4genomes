Sidney WYNNE, 3rd child of Dr. Thomas WYNNE and Martha BUTTALL [Family], was born about 1666 in Bronvendog, Flintshire, Wales. Sidney died about 1709 in West River, Anne Arundel, Maryland aged about 43.
